### 🎓 The Professional: Cyber Security & Networks
As a Cyber Security and Networks student, I am focused on the rigorous fundamentals of network architecture, infrastructure defense, and secure systems. I treat my academic and theoretical work with deep professionalism, building a solid foundation in how data moves, how networks operate, and how to protect them from the ground up.

### 🛠️ The Tinkerer: Homelab & Hardware Chaos
When the textbooks close, the server rack powers on! I absolutely love getting my hands dirty with custom IoT, wiring up ESP32s/ESP8266s, and building local-only smart home ecosystems with Home Assistant. Whether I am spinning up virtual machines on Proxmox, soldering custom PCBs, or containerizing services in Docker, my homelab is my sandbox. It is where I build things, break them, and figure out how to put them back together.

### 🤖 AI-Driven Workflow
I consider myself an intermediate developer who believes in working smart. I actively leverage AI tools as my pair-programmers to architect solutions, accelerate my coding workflow, and troubleshoot complex bugs. Using AI allows me to punch above my weight class, build more ambitious projects efficiently, and continuously learn new logic and frameworks without getting stuck in the weeds.
